 About Our Club

Computer technology is changing everyday.   In ancient time, our club was more focus on hardware.   Past few years ago, we were more concern on software and STEM.   However, recently students are more interested on eSport.    Therefore we have started to introduce eSport competition at school since 2019.    Besides of eSport, we also find that drone programming is the trend for young people.   We are going to provide the drone programming course in future.

 About Our Club

Computer technology is changing every day. In ancient times, our club was more focused on hardware. Past few years ago, we were more concerned about software and STEM. However, recently students are more interested in eSport. Therefore we have started to introduce the eSport competition at school since 2019. Besides of eSport, we also find that drone programming is the trend for young people. We are going to provide the drone programming course in the future.
